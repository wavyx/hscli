      <p>JSON output, deterministic exit codes, and a raw <code>hscli api</code> hatch let agents and pipelines drive Help Scout directly.</p>

    <article class="feature-card">
      <div class="feature-card__icon"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.9" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><path d="M3 12h7l2-3 3 6 2-3h4"/></svg></div>
      <h3>Pipe-friendly output</h3>
      <p>Every command supports <code>--output table|json|yaml|csv</code>, plus <code>--jq</code> and <code>--fields</code> for inline filtering.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="feature-card">
      <div class="feature-card__icon"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.9" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><path d="M12 2l8 3v6c0 5-3.5 8.5-8 11-4.5-2.5-8-6-8-11V5z"/><path d="M9 12l2 2 4-4"/></svg></div>
      <h3>Secure by default</h3>
      <p>OAuth tokens live only in your OS keychain — never written to disk in plaintext. <code>hscli api</code> is locked to the Help Scout host.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="feature-card">
      <div class="feature-card__icon"><svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.9" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"><ellipse cx="12" cy="5" rx="8" ry="3"/><path d="M4 5v6c0 1.7 3.6 3 8 3s8-1.3 8-3V5"/><path d="M4 11v6c0 1.7 3.6 3 8 3s8-1.3 8-3v-6"/></svg></div>
      <h3>Full account backups</h3>
      <p><code>hscli backup</code> dumps your whole account to JSON with incremental sync, resume, deletion detection, and attachments.</p>
    </article>
